About Me

A native of Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, Kira graduated from William Smith College with a BA in English. She is passionate about art and technology and how the two intersect, and wishes to contribute to the ongoing communications between people and computers. After moving to Southern California, she decided to follow her curiousity into UX/UI.

  • She has had two short stories published, Strands of Gold and Her Poems are Inked in Fears and Blood.
  • She has nine years of customer service experience, so she knows how to work quickly under pressure and as part of a team.
  • She can play guitar very poorly.
